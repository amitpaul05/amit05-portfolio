After building the logging foundation yesterday, today was mostly about making sure important business actions were actually being captured.

I extended audit logging across evidence submissions, review workflows, certifications, Stripe payment events, and document management. While mapping the flows, I noticed a few events that did not have a real implementation path yet, such as certificate suspension and auditor assignment. Instead of adding placeholder logging calls, I left them out to avoid creating misleading audit records.

I also worked on log retention. Since audit data can grow quickly, I added automated cleanup using a scheduled Celery task and management command, with retention periods varying based on data sensitivity.

Finished the day by adding automated tests around the logging service and retention flow. The extra test coverage gave me confidence that future feature work won't silently break the audit trail.
