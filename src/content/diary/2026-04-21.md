Before the grind officially starts, I want to write down *why* I'm doing this.

Big tech isn't just the brand. It's the caliber of the problems, the quality of the people around you, and the career optionality that comes after. I want to be the kind of engineer who can walk into any room and contribute meaningfully.

**What I know I need:**
- Algorithms & data structures — solid but needs sharpening under pressure
- System design — currently at "I've read about it" level, needs to reach "I've internalized it"
- Communication — I tend to over-engineer explanations. Practice conciseness.

**The plan:**
- 2 LeetCode problems every day minimum. Focus on patterns, not memorization.
- 1 chapter of DDIA per week.
- Weekly mock interviews starting next month.

Starting tomorrow. No more "I'll start Monday."
